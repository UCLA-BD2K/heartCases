This is an open access article distributed under the Creative Commons Attribution License, which permits unrestricted use, distribution, and
reproduction in any medium, provided the original work is properly cited. We report the case of a 63 year old woman who developed the
complications of cholangitis and Budd Chiari syndrome secondary to polycystic disease of the liver. The two complications were not present
simultaneously, and both resolved after decompression of the liver cysts.
