reproduction in any medium, provided the original work is properly cited. Cystic lesions of the pancreas are relatively uncommon. We
describe the case of a young man with a complex cystic mass located within the head of the pancreas. The patient underwent exploration with
resection of the mass. Pathology revealed a ciliated epithelial cyst, a rare cystic lesion of the pancreas.
