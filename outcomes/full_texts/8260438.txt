reproduction in any medium, provided the original work is properly cited. Acute acalculous cholecystitis may develop in patients suffering
from necrotizing pancreatitis. Conversely, acute pancreatitis may complicate acute gallbladder disease. We present a case that lends support
to the existence of another possibility: gallbladder necrosis caused by direct extension of the necrotizing pancreatitic process.
