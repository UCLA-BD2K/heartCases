reproduction in any medium, provided the original work is properly cited. We present a case of adult hepatoblastoma. This young female
presented with severe acute cholangitis. Preoperative diagnosis was common bile duct (CBD) obstruction with portal vein thrombosis. On
exploration she had a tumor mass in the CBD. The unusual features of this case are discussed in this report.
