reproduction in any medium, provided the original work is properly cited. A case of metastatic granulosa cell tumour of the ovary is
reported. Investigations revealed a secondary tumour in segment VI and VII of the liver. Right hepatic resection was performed. Microscopic
findings revealed a tumour with histological features identical to that removed eleven years before.
