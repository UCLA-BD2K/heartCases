reproduction in any medium, provided the original work is properly cited. Duodenal injury following blunt abdominal trauma is uncommon. The
severity of injury can vary from an intramural haematoma to a duodenal rupture with associated transection of the pancreatic duct. A case of
duodenal rupture with avulsion of the ampulla of Vater is presented and discussed.
