reproduction in any medium, provided the original work is properly cited. Background: Patients with unilateral ovarian abscesses due to
Salmonella are rare. Case report: A 48-year-old woman with a left ovarian abscess caused by Salmonella group O7 is reported. Conclusion: In
our patient, the ovary may have been seeded hematogenously by salmonellae and may have evolved into a local infection.
