reproduction in any medium, provided the original work is properly cited. A patient is presented with multiple vascular anomalies in the
branches of the celiac axis as well as in the portal vein and its branches. Apparently, unique in the literature is the presence of a large
arteriovenous fistula between the hepatic artery and one of the hepatic veins. The anomalies are presumed to be congenital in origin.
