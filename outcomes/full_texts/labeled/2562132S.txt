reproduction in any medium, provided the original work is properly cited. A case of fibrous histiocytoma of low grade malignancy arising
from the uncinate lobe of the pancreas is reported. This is an unusual site for these extremely rare tumours. Survival up to 4 years has
been achieved in our patient following surgical resection.
