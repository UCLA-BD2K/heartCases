reproduction in any medium, provided the original work is properly cited. A 65 year old patient with polycystic liver disease presented with
obstructive jaundice thought to be a cholangiocarcinoma. Subsequent investigations demonstrated a large cyst compressing the confluence of
the hepatic ducts. Percutaneous decompression of the biliary tree led to a complication necessitating surgery. Treatment options for
symptomatic polycystic liver disease are reviewed.
