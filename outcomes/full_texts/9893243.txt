This is an open access article distributed under the Creative Commons Attribution License, which permits unrestricted use, distribution, and
reproduction in any medium, provided the original work is properly cited. We report a case of lymphoepithelial cyst of the pancreas with
non-specific elevation of CA 19.9 and CEA. Pre-operative diagnosis by conventional means proved elusive, and only surgical resection and
histopathology revealed the diagnosis. The origin and diagnosis are discussed by literature review.
